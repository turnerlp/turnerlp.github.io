DIY- Pillow Centerpiece

Come up with unique centerpieces to spice up an event. Here's how to create these beautiful pillow centerpieces.

Supplies

  • White satin material- 2 yards
  • White pearls (flat back)
  • Clear rhinestones- 1 spool
  • Purple ribbons- 1" and 1/8"
  • Purple tulle- 1 spool
  • Cotton filling
  • Styrofoam- 12" square
  • Calla lillies book marks (in cases)
  • Hot glue gun
  • Scissor
  • Custom made stickers (zazzle.com)
